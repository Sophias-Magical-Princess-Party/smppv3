Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

What area do you serve? +

We proudly serve the South Houston area and surrounding communities. Call us to confirm your specific location when booking.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for weekend dates which fill up quickly. Try not to book more than 3 months in advance. Give us a call to check availability, last minute requests are not guaranteed!

How does payment work? +

A $50 deposit is required to hold you time/date. You may send the remaining balance with your deposit or at any time before the day of your party. We will not arrive until full payment has been received. Invoices are made using Square, we do not accept cash for bookings, however cash tips are appreciated.

Do you offer video messages? +

Yes! Personalized video messages from your chosen princess are available. Perfect for birthdays, holidays, or just a magical surprise. Prices begin at $50 or reach out for a direct quote.

Does The Party Have To Be At My House? +

No! During the booking process we can recommend our favorite venues based on your location and needs.

What do I need to prepare? +

We are an entertainment service only. We will not provide anything else such as decorations, food or photography, so please plan accordingly.
